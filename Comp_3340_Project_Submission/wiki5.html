How to Change Site Theme

Within the Homepage, a user may use the "Standard View" dropdown to select a different theme for their current view of the main page. There are three views available: standard-view, bulking-view, and cutting-view. Each view gives a slightly different feel to the site, corresponding to the different stages of a fitness journey(bulking and cutting), and is an added bonus to our users.
